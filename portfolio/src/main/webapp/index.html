Welcome to my portfolio!

I am a first generation college student studying Computer Science at Michigan State University! I plan on having a cognate in Math as well as a minor in Computational Mathematics Science and Engineering which focuses on data science techniques - numbers and graphs are cool.

My journey to majoring in Computer Science was not a straight path; I originally planned to major in Mechanical Engineering, then after participating in Google's CSSI I fell in love with Computer Science and made the switch, then it was Physics after discovering fascinating research opportunities using computing, and then back to Computer Science once I realized it was my true passion after being a TA for a Computer Science summer program.

My two favorite on campus jobs I have had so far have been being a TA and a Research Assistant. I was a TA for a Computer Science program through MSU's Engineering Science Success Academy! The Computer Science portion of the program focused on preparing incoming freshmen for their first intro CS course at MSU by teaching them the basics of Python. My next favorite job was my Research Assistant position at the National Superconducting Cyclotron Lab on MSU's campus; there I would perform data analytics on tin isotope collisions using C++ for a Nuclear Physicist.

While school work keeps me busy, I do participate in three on campus organizations: the figure skating team, women in computing, and Phi Sigma Rho Sorority for women in engineering and engineering technology!

My current interests in Computer Science are Machine Learning, Neural Networks, and Data Analytics. I love Computer Science becuase you can do so many different things with it from web development to building an image recognition model! I think my long-term career goal is to be an outreach director because I am quite passionate about teaching, inspiring, encouraging, and sharing my knowledge with students of all ages - I want every person to have an opportunity to get involved in Computer Science and Tech!! :)
